Conservation Trust Fund Estimates for Special Districts

The Department of Local Affairs distributes Conservation Trust Fund (CTF) dollars quarterly, on a per capita basis, to over 470 eligible local governments: counties, cities, towns, and Title 32 special districts that provide park and recreation services in their service plans.

The program is administered by the Division of Local Government. Information about the program can be found on the DOLA Conservation Trust Fund Estimates Program Page

The State Demography Office prepares the annual population estimates for the counties, cities, towns, and Title 32 special districts. The population estimates for the Title 32 special districts that participate in the CTF are available below.

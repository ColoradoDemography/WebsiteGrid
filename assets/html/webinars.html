2025 Webinar Series

The 2025 State Demography Office Webinar Series presents lunchtime presentations focusing on SDO and U.S. Census Bureau data resources, analysis use cases, and key topics to help you understand how Colorado's population characteristics impact your programs.

These webinars are free, but registration is required. All webinar presentation slides, example documents, and recordings will be posted on this page.

Have an idea for a future webinar? Please send your suggestions to SDO by clicking on the "Questions? Contact SDO" button on the left-hand side of the page.

February 2025: Introduction to the SDO Website and American Community Survey Resources

Date and Time: Tuesday, February 4, 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m.

This webinar presents an overview of the SDO Website and the American Community Survey (ACS) resources maintained by SDO, including

  • Data Access Tools
    • SDO Census Data API
    • data.census.gov: ACS Data Portal
  • SDO ACS Spreadsheets
    • Additional Geographies: Counties, Municiplaities, and Census Designated Places (CDP)
    • Housing Occupancy and Vacancy Data
    • Language Spoken at Home for Individuals and Households
    • Work Location and Travel Time
    • Ancestry
  • ACS Thematic and Interactive Maps
